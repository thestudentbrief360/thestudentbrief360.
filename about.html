OUR STORY

Building a place where student ideas are taken seriously.

The Student Brief 360 is a student led educational media platform designed to turn complex issues into clear, thoughtful, and accessible content written by students for students.

Why We Started

Students today are surrounded by more information than ever before. Yet much of it feels inaccessible, overly complex, or disconnected from student experiences. We created Student Brief 360 to make educational writing more approachable and to create space for students to publish ideas that matter.

What Makes Us Different

Every article begins with a student voice. Our briefs are designed to be concise, readable, and engaging while still encouraging research, critical thinking, and thoughtful discussion.
