The Problem We're Solving
Students today are surrounded by more information than ever before. Yet most of it is either too complex, too biased, or simply not designed with students in mind.

Information Overload

Students constantly move between news, social media, academic work, and opinion content. Finding trustworthy information becomes exhausting.

Why It Matters

Without digestible resources, students may disengage entirely or build understanding from incomplete information.
